How TikTag works in command-line:

{{< card-code lang="Shell" >}} Host an asset, > tiktag myfilename.png > Tik...success! Tag...your asset is hosted at, > https://s3.tikoly.com/village/563583552944996352.png Retrieve an asset's URL, > tiktag -s myfilename.png > We found this asset at, > https://s3.tikoly.com/village/563583552944996352.png {{< /card-code>}}

Tag...you share HOW you handled them.
