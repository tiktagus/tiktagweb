We use TikTag everyday.

{{< /blocks/cover >}} {{% blocks/lead %}} Before you mint your works as NFT, Non-Fungible Token,
you either store them on disk (locally) or somewhere you trust on the cloud. That's fine. {{% /blocks/lead %}} {{< blocks/section >}}

... UNTIL you want people to trust you before they buy.

{{< /blocks/section >}} {{< blocks/section >}}

TikTag adds more transparency to
how you're keeping your digital assets.


{{% blocks/feature icon="fa fa-fingerprint" title="Globally Unique Asset ID" %}} When you run `tiktag myfile.png`, we assign a globally unique ID to your asset, while you decide where to store them. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa fa-floppy-disk" title="You Keep Your Asset" %}} You store image/asset locally or on cloud storage of your choice, until you feel right to mint their NFT. We keep NO asset in the original. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{% blocks/feature icon="fa fa-shoe-prints" title="Opt-in Track & Traceability" %}} You decide how much you want others to know about your asset's `meta info`, such as time created or minted and how. {{% /blocks/feature %}} {{< /blocks/section >}}
